How to Use the App

Welcome! Follow these simple steps to get started with our app:

  1. Create an account: Click the Sign Up button on the home page and fill in your details.
  2. Verify your email: Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to confirm your account.
  3. Log in: Use your email and password to access your dashboard.
  4. Explore features: Use the navigation bar to browse through different sections like Profile, Settings, and Reports.
  5. Get help: Click the Help menu for FAQs or contact support directly.

Tips for Best Experience

  • Keep your profile information updated.
  • Use the latest version of your browser.
  • Enable notifications to stay updated.
